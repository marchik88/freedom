product storage

Sunflower and soya meals are kept in warehouses equipped with supply and exhaust ventilation systems. The rooms have sufficient air flow, are clean, dry, not infested by bread damaging insects, protected from the sunlight and heating. The meal produced is stored in bulk or in bags.

Sunflower oil is stored in special reservoirs designed for this purpose following all norms and rules of keeping food products. The tanks and reservoirs for keeping oil are cleaned in terms required by normative documents (not less than once per year).
